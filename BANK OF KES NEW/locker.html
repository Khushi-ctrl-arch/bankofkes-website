Locker:

1 . Visit the Bank:

Visit your preferred bank branch where you wish to apply for a locker. It's advisable to inquire beforehand if the branch offers locker facilities.

2. Request Application Form:

: Approach the customer service or relationship manager at the bank and request an application form for a locker.

3. Complete Application Form:

Fill out the application form with accurate details. You will need to provide personal information such as your name, address, contact details, and possibly some financial information.

4. Submit Required Documents:

: Along with the application form, you'll need to submit certain documents which may include: - Proof of identity (passport, driver's license, Aadhaar card, etc.) - Proof of address (utility bills, rental agreement, etc.) - Passport-size photographs (usually 2 or 3) - Any other documents as specified by the bank

5. Choose Locker Size:

Select the size of the locker you require based on availability and your needs. Locker sizes vary from bank to bank, with smaller lockers suitable for storing documents and larger ones for storing valuables or larger items.

6. Pay Fees:

: Pay the applicable fees for renting the locker. This typically includes an annual rental fee and possibly a refundable deposit.

7. Locker Allocation:

Once your application is processed and approved by the bank, you will be allocated a locker based on availability.

8. Sign Agreement:

Sign the locker rental agreement provided by the bank. This agreement outlines the terms and conditions of using the locker, including access timings, responsibilities, and liabilities.

9. Receive Access Details:

After completing all formalities, the bank will provide you with the keys or access card to your locker. You may also receive instructions on how to access the locker and operate it securely.

10. Store Items:

Start using the locker to store your valuables or important documents securely.
