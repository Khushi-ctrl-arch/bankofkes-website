Passbook:

Steps:

1.Choose a Bank:

Select a bank where you want to open the passbook account. Consider factors like proximity, reputation, services offered, and fees.

2.Visit the Bank Branch:

Go to the nearest branch of the chosen bank where you wish to open the account.

3.Account Opening Form:

Obtain an account opening form from the bank. You may need to fill out details such as your name, address, occupation, contact information, and nominee details.

4.Provide KYC Documents:

Submit the following documents along with the filled form: i.Proof of identity (PAN card, Aadhaar card, passport, driver's license, etc.) ii.Proof of address (Aadhaar card, passport, utility bills, etc.) iii.Passport-sized photographs

5.Initial Deposit:

Deposit the minimum initial amount required by the bank to open a savings account. This amount varies depending on the bank and type of account

6.Signature and Photograph:

Provide your signature and photographs as required on the account opening form and other documents.

7.Nomination:

You may be asked to nominate a beneficiary for the account. This is optional but recommended for joint accounts.

8.Receive Passbook:

Once your application and documents are verified and processed by the bank, you will be issued a passbook. The passbook contains details of your account transactions, withdrawals, deposits, and balance.

9.Activate ATM Card (if applicable):

If you opt for an ATM card linked to your savings account, it will be issued separately. You may need to activate it before use.

10.Receive Welcome Kit:

You may receive a welcome kit containing additional information about your account, including terms and condition.
