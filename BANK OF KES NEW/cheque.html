Cheque deposit:

Steps:

1.Endorsement:

Sign the back of the cheque. This ensures it can only be deposited into your account.

2.Visit Bank or ATM:

Go to your bank or an ATM that accepts cheque deposits.

3.Fill out Deposit Slip (if applicable):

If depositing at the bank counter, complete a deposit slip with your account number and the amount.

4.Submit the Cheque:

Hand over the cheque and any required forms to the bank teller or insert it into the ATM.

5.Confirmation:

Keep the receipt or transaction record as proof of deposit.

6.Processing:

The bank will process the cheque, which may take a few business days.
