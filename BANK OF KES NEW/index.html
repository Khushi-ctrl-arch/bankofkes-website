You Initiate We Continue..

On July 2, 1985, Dr. L.Bhushan founded the institution with a vision for growth and excellance, also serving as it's primary promoter. At it's inception, the organisation operated with a modest network of 12 branches, reflecting it's early stage yet ambitious aspiration.

Over subsequent decades, the institution experienced remarkable expansion. Guided by Dr.Bhushan and successive leadership, it now boast an extensive network of 3294 branches. This growth highlights the strategic vision, dedicated efforts, and unwavering determination that have driven the institution to it's current stature.
